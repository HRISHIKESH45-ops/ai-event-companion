Ever been to a massive hackathon and felt paralyzed by the hundreds of overlapping workshops and speaker sessions? Me too. That's why I built my submission for the Hack2Skill Virtual #PromptWars: **AI Event Companion**! 🗓️⚡

Unlike static scheduling apps, this is an agentic, multi-modal web app built entirely with Next.js and the incredibly fast Google Gemini APIs (@GoogleAI). 

Here are the "God-Tier" features under the hood:

🖼️ **Zero-Friction Vision**: Too lazy to type? Drag and drop a picture of a hackathon flyer into the system. The Gemini Vision model extracts session times, titles, and dates automatically.
🔥 **Energy Drain Heatmap**: The backend AI intelligently algorithmically grades each activity (1-10) and renders a visual heatmap so you don't burn out.
⚡ **Dynamic Tweak Copilot**: Schedules change. So I built an AI chat copilot directly into the timeline. Ask it to "add a nap at 2 PM", and it hot-swaps the strictly-typed JSON schedule in place.
🛡️ **Hackathon-resilient Architecture**: Implemented a multi-model failover system (`gemini-2.5-flash` cascading down to `gemini-2.0-flash-lite`) alongside a secure Bring-Your-Own-Key structure to ensure 100% demo uptime.
